Moment of Resistance of Balanced Section

This page explains how to calculate the moment of resistance (MoR) for a balanced reinforced concrete section.

The formula used is:

$$ M_{u,lim} = 0.36 \, f_{ck} \, b \, x_u \left( d - 0.42 \, x_u \right) $$

Where:

  • fck = Characteristic compressive strength of concrete
  • b = Width of section
  • xu = Neutral axis depth
  • d = Effective depth of section
