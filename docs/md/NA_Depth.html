Neutral Axis Depth Calculator (RC Member)

This page explains how to calculate the depth of the neutral axis for a balanced section in reinforced concrete.

The formula used is:

$$ \frac{x_{u,max}}{d} = \frac{0.36 \, f_{ck}} { 0.87 \, f_y \, \left(\frac{A_{st}}{b \, d}\right)} $$

Here:

  • fck = Characteristic compressive strength of concrete
  • fy = Yield strength of steel
  • Ast = Area of tensile reinforcement
  • b = Width of section
  • d = Effective depth
